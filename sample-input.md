# Sample Input

Create a long-form Hashnode article in English about the new phase of Artificial Intelligence, where AI is evolving into standalone apps, universal assistants, enterprise platforms, and autonomous agents.

The article should start from the observation that app stores are now full of AI applications focused on very specific tasks, such as text-to-speech, speech-to-text, document analysis, PDF summarization, image generation, voice tools, writing assistants, and productivity apps.

The central message should be that AI is not moving toward one single dominant application. It is becoming a layer inside everything. For personal users, AI appears as simple apps. For companies, AI needs security, governance, identity management, compliance, APIs, integrations, observability, and workflow orchestration.

Also explain how tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and DeepSeek are becoming more than chatbots. They are becoming interfaces for agents, automation, APIs, workflows, and knowledge work.

Make the article optimistic but realistic. Mention that many simple AI apps may disappear, but the demand for AI-powered workflows, agents, and enterprise automation will continue to grow.

End by reinforcing that professionals in IT support, cloud, automation, data, and AI should start learning how apps, agents, cloud platforms, APIs, and workflows connect to create real business value.
